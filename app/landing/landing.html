About Us

Quiet friendly Community

A best kept secret in Fort Gratiot, MI. Tucked back behind retail space, residents enjoy the quietness of this community while also being centrally located to all Fort Gratiot has to offer

Low Maintenance

Lawn maintenance, snow removal and garbage are taken care of by the community management tean which means less headaches for you!

Controlled Fees

Lot fees are set at $285/month and are ONLY adjusted based on cost of living changes

Aesthetic Upgrades

Birchwood Estates West was developed with looks in mind. All homes in the community are newer than 2011, have attached garages and block skirting and sit on larger than normal lots.

Flexibility

You have the option of purchasing a pre-owned home or custom ordering a home that fits your life style. We work with you to ensure you are happy with your new home.

Family Owned & Operated

Birchwood Estates West has been family owned and operated since its Grand Opening in 2002.
